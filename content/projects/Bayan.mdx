Bayan is a preparation tool for khateebs and scholars. It helps with the research parts of khutbah prep by searching the Quran corpus by meaning, checking verse references, and generating an outline from a topic.

The system is meant to assist, not replace. It does not write the khutbah itself. It helps users prepare faster while keeping their own words and reasoning.
