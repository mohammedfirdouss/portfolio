# Terra Towns

This section provides a technical diagram and explanation of the Terra Towns project.

![Terra Towns Diagram](/terratowns.png)
*Figure 1: High-level architecture of the Terra Towns project, showcasing cloud-native components for sustainable urban environments.*

## Overview (Cloud Engineer Perspective)

The Terra Towns diagram illustrates a robust, cloud-native architecture for deploying and managing sustainable urban environments. It leverages AWS CloudFront for content delivery, S3 for static website hosting, and Terraform Cloud for infrastructure as code. This setup ensures high availability, scalability, and efficient deployment of the urban infrastructure's digital twin.

## Importance

This architecture is crucial for managing complex smart city initiatives, enabling rapid iteration on urban planning, and providing a scalable platform for citizen engagement and data collection. Its infrastructure-as-code approach ensures consistency and disaster recovery capabilities.
