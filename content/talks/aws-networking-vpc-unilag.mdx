I presented on AWS networking essentials at AWS Cloud Club UNILAG, with a focus on VPC and connectivity.

## What I Covered

- **VPC fundamentals** — Virtual Private Clouds, CIDR blocks, and IP addressing
- **Subnets and connectivity** — Public vs private subnets, NAT gateways, and internet access
- **Security groups** — Inbound and outbound rules, stateful filtering
- **Connectivity patterns** — VPC peering, VPN, and hybrid connectivity
