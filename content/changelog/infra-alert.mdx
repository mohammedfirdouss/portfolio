# InfraAlert v0.1.0

I'm excited to share what I've been working on: **InfraAlert**. 

As a cloud engineer, I often found existing monitoring solutions either too complex for small projects or too expensive. InfraAlert is my attempt to bridge that gap.

## Key Features

- **Multi-Cloud Support**: Works with AWS, Azure, and GCP.
- **Real-time Notifications**: Get alerts instantly on Slack, Discord, or via Email.
- **Lightweight**: Minimal footprint, easy to deploy as a container or serverless function.
- **Customizable Thresholds**: Define what "critical" means for your specific workload.

## Why I built this

The goal was to create something that I could drop into any environment and have basic health checks running in minutes. No complex dashboards, just the data you need when things go wrong.
