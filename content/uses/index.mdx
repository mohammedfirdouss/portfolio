### Hardware
*   **Dell Latitude 3600**: My reliable daily driver for development.

### IDEs & Editors
*   **Kiro**: A game-changer primarily for its **Specs Feature**, which significantly streamlines my workflow.
*   **Cursor**: My go-to AI-powered code editor.
*   **Anigravity**: Another powerful editor in my rotation.

### Terminal & CLIs
*   **Gemini CLI**: Integrating Google's AI directly into my command line workflow.
*   **Cursor CLI**: Seamlessly launching and managing my editor sessions.
*   **Warp Terminal**: AI-integrated terminal that makes bash easier.
*   **Oh My Zsh**: With the `powerlevel10k` theme for git stats at a glance.

### Cloud & DevOps
*   **AWS**: My home cloud. Lambda, DynamoDB, and API Gateway are my bread and butter.
*   **Terraform**: Infrastructure as Code. I don't click buttons in the console.
*   **Docker**: If it works on my machine, it works everywhere.
*   **GitHub Actions**: Automating everything from tests to deployments.

### Productivity
*   **Notion**: My second brain. Project tracking, documentation, and life organization.
*   **Raycast**: Replaces Spotlight. It's a launcher, calculator, and script runner.
*   **Linear**: For tracking issues and features in my personal projects.
